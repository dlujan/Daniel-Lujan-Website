About

Hey there! I'm Daniel, a full-stack software developer with a passion for creating. I'm a graduate of Baylor University where I actually got my Master's in Music Composition. If you're interested in hearing how I went from music to tech check out my blog! When I'm not coding I enjoy staying active, baking banana bread, and playing tabletop games with my friends.

I first started coding by making games in Unity and I've never looked back. I've learned that with code anything is possible and I'm always looking for opportunities to learn more, create something new, and become a better developer. For me, the fun is in solving difficult problems, building ideas into realities, and providing people with something that enhances their lives. I specialize in full-stack JavaScript development and frequently work with tools such as React, Vue, Node, Nest.js, and PostreSQL.
